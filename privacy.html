HOW WE UTILIZE YOUR INFORMATION AND WHEN WE MAY SHARE IT

By providing your consent, we may utilize and/or disclose your Personal Information for the following primary purposes: (a) promoting our business to you; (b) addressing your inquiries; (c) assisting with payment processing for the products or services you purchase; (d) sending you additional materials related to Kailash Waste Solutions, including products and services that may be of interest to you; and (e) providing effective customer service.

2.2 Additionally, Kailash Waste Solutions may use and/or disclose Personal Information for purposes other than our primary objectives (secondary purposes). With your consent, we may use and/or disclose your Personal Information for the following secondary purposes: (a) billing and account management; (b) business planning and product development; (c) gaining a better understanding of your needs and interests; (d) enhancing the content, functionality, and usability of our website; (e) improving our marketing and promotional efforts; (f) displaying personalized advertising when you visit our website; (g) fulfilling any other purpose identified in any other agreement between Kailash and you; (h) providing relevant information about issues, news, or updates related to your interactions with Kailash Waste Solutions, or about Kailash Waste Solutions in general, or the industries in which either you or Kailash operates; (i) in cases where there are reasonable suspicions of unlawful activity, using or disclosing personal information as necessary for investigating the matter or reporting concerns to relevant individuals or authorities; (j) re-engaging with you and sending marketing materials (including via email, telephone, or SMS) if your account has become inactive; and (k) as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.

2.3 Use of Cookies - Besides the secondary purposes outlined above, we utilize Google Analytics (a web analytics service provided by Google) and gather data such as age, gender, and browsing activity to gain insights into our customers' behavior and provide targeted advertising tailored to their interests and preferences. Therefore, when you visit our website, we, and/or a third party, may place cookies on your browser to collect information such as your computer's location, browsing activity, and time spent on our website. This information helps us measure and research the effectiveness of website features and offers, as well as serve relevant ads to our users. By using this website and agreeing to this policy, you grant us permission to place and store cookies on your browser. You can manage cookies by adjusting your individual browser settings. You can also opt-out of receiving personalized ads by clicking here or here. Please note that if you choose to opt-out, you will still see online advertisements, but they will be less relevant to your interests.

2.4 Kailash Waste Solutions may use and/or disclose your Personal Information for any secondary purposes not explicitly mentioned in this Privacy Policy if: (a) the secondary purpose is related to a primary purpose; and (b) you would reasonably expect us to use the information for that secondary purpose.

2.5 On occasion, we may disclose your Personal Information to external organizations in order to provide the services you require. Your Personal Information is shared with these organizations solely for the purpose of us delivering our services to you. Among other services, these organizations carry out: (a) billing and debt recovery functions; (b) customer inquiries; (c) information technology services; (d) marketing services (including market research); (e) website usage analysis; and (f) supporting or facilitating any activities described in Points 2.1 and 2.2 of this Privacy Policy. 2.6 Our website utilizes social plugins (referred to as "Plugins") from the social network Facebook, operated by Facebook Inc., 1601 S. California Ave, Palo Alto, CA 94304, USA (referred to as "Facebook"). The plugins are identified by a Facebook logo or the words "Social Plugin by Facebook" or "Facebook Social Plugin". When you visit a page on our website that contains a plugin, your browser establishes a direct connection to Facebook servers. Facebook directly transmits the plugin content to your browser, which is then incorporated into the website. By integrating the plugin, Facebook receives information that your browser has accessed the page on our website, even if you don't have a Facebook account or are not logged into Facebook at the time. This information, including your IP address, is sent directly from your browser to a Facebook server in the United States and stored there. If you are logged into Facebook, Facebook can directly associate your visit to our website with your Facebook account. If you interact with the plugins, such as pressing the "Like" button or leaving a comment, the corresponding information is also transmitted directly to a Facebook server and stored there. This information is also posted on Facebook and can be seen by your Facebook friends. Facebook may use this information for advertising, market research, and personalized design of Facebook pages. To prevent Facebook from linking the data collected via our website to your Facebook account, you must log out of Facebook before visiting our website. For more information about the purpose and extent of Facebook's data collection, processing, and usage, as well as your rights and available privacy settings, please review the Facebook privacy policy.

10. GLOSSARY

"Collect" refers to the act of gathering, acquiring, or obtaining information through lawful and fair means, where the individual's identity is identifiable or identified.

"Direct Marketing" involves the utilization and/or disclosure of Personal Information to directly communicate with an individual for the purpose of promoting goods or services, using written, verbal, or electronic methods of communication (including but not limited to phone, SMS, and email). The goods or services being marketed may include those offered by Kailash Waste Solutions, its Related Body Corporates, or independent third-party organizations.

"Disclosure" generally denotes the sharing of information outside of Kailash Waste Solutions, including instances where it is contracted to carry out an "outsourced function."

"Express Consent Consequences" encompass the understanding that Kailash Waste Solutions will not be held accountable under the applicable Privacy Act, and individuals will not have recourse under the Privacy Act if they provide consent for the disclosure of their Personal Information to an overseas recipient, who subsequently mishandles the Personal Information in violation of the Privacy Act.

"Expressly Informed" refers to the circumstance in which Kailash Waste Solutions has clearly communicated, either verbally or in writing, the consequences of providing express consent.

"Opt Out" represents an individual's explicit request to no longer receive further Direct Marketing communications.

"Personal Information" pertains to information, excluding Sensitive Information, that relates to an identifiable or identified individual. It includes, but is not limited to, an individual's name, address, phone number, email address, or any other information as outlined in this policy, regardless of its format or recorded form.

"Primary Purpose" denotes the main reason for collecting Personal Information, as described in clause 2.1 of this policy.

"Reasonable Expectation" signifies the expectation that an ordinary individual would reasonably have regarding the Use or Disclosure of their personal information for a particular purpose.

"Related Body Corporate" carries the same meaning as defined in the Companies Act 2013.

"Secondary Purpose" refers to a purpose of Use or Disclosure other than the Primary Purpose, as detailed in clause 2.2 of this policy.

"Sensitive Information" holds the same meaning as defined in the Information Technology Act 2000.

"Use" denotes the handling of Personal Information within Kailash Waste Solutions.

"Website" encompasses the website, mobile app, and related webpages owned or operated by Kailash Waste Solutions, as well as any other website and its related webpages owned or operated by Kailash Waste Solutions.
